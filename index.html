General Opjective!

The general objective of this research is to provide and to produce an integrated approach that uses GIS\RS techniques to identify optimal land and water sources for investment in mixed crop-livestock production.





Specific objectives:



1. To use GIS/RS in the delineation of potential locations of arable land.

2. To use GIS/RS in identifying the optimal sites for establishing reservoirs to harvest rain water.

3. To produce a map of potential arable land in the study area.

4. To produce a map of optimal sites for establishing rainwater harvesting reservoirs in the study area.

5. To estimate the area of arable land in the study area.

6. To estimate the amount of water that can be stored behind the reservoir.

7. To estimate the area of land that can be irrigated by the rain water behind the reservoir.
